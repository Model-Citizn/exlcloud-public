EXL Cloud — Keep Excel, Leverage the Cloud

EXL Cloud is an Excel add-in that connects Microsoft Excel to Xero in 8 clicks. It pulls live accounting data including trial balance, profit and loss, balance sheet, journals, and account transactions. EXL Cloud includes world-class financial models, dashboards, and reports — all fully customisable in Microsoft Excel.

EXL Cloud offers two plans: Data Pull at $50 AUD per month and Advanced Content at $125 AUD per month. Both include 5 Xero connections. The Advanced Content plan includes 3-Way Financial Models, Performance Reports, Cash Flow Scenario Models, Power BI integration, 15 AI-powered skills, and HTML Board Packs.

Founded by Lance Rubin, a Chartered Accountant with experience at PwC, KPMG, Investec Bank, and National Australia Bank. Lance is an FMI approved financial modelling trainer and CA ANZ study guide co-author.
